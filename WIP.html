Grading Guide

New
Brand new and unopened. The item and packaging are in perfect condition with no defects or signs of wear.

A Grade – Like New
Opened but unused. The item looks brand new and shows no signs of use. Packaging may have been unsealed but remains in excellent condition.

B Grade – Great
Lightly used and fully checked. The item is complete and in great condition, with only very minor signs of use or wear to the packaging.

C Grade – Good
Used but fully functional and complete. There may be visible wear or minor cosmetic damage to the box or components, which will be described in detail.

D Grade – Acceptable
Well-used or older items that still function as intended. There may be noticeable wear, replacement parts, or box damage. Typically applies to vintage or hard-to-find items.

For Parts / Incomplete
Items that are missing components or not fully functional. The description will clearly state what’s missing or affected.

Delivery Information

We want you to always know where your order is — and to get it quickly!

That’s why we include free tracked delivery via Royal Mail Tracked 48 on most products (unless otherwise specified).

We’ll always do our best to offer a free option. For some smaller or lower-cost items, we may charge for tracked delivery, but we’ll make sure to offer Royal Mail 2nd Class as a free alternative. We always recommend choosing tracked delivery if possible — it helps us resolve any delivery issues quickly — but we think it’s only fair to give you the choice! Tracked delivery is selected by default, so if you’d prefer 2nd Class, please make sure to choose it at checkout.

How Quick Is Delivery?

Most orders sent with Tracked 48 arrive within 2–4 working days.

Need it sooner? You can upgrade to Royal Mail Tracked 24 at checkout for delivery within 1–2 working days.

International Orders: Free shipping applies to UK orders only. For international buyers, please check the postage section on the listing for estimated costs. Dispatch times remain the same, but delivery speed will vary depending on your location. If you have any questions, feel free to get in touch — we’re happy to help.

When Will My Order Be Dispatched?

We dispatch orders Monday to Saturday. Place your order before 6pm for next-day dispatch.

Orders placed after 6pm on Friday will be sent on Monday (excluding Bank Holidays).

Can I Collect?

Are you local? We’re happy to arrange collection if you prefer — just get in touch before placing your order and we’ll be glad to help.

Returns Policy

Damaged, Faulty or Incorrect Items

If your item arrives damaged, faulty, or incorrect, please contact us as soon as possible and let us know what’s happened. It’s always best to send us a message first, as we may be able to resolve the issue without needing a return. If you believe your item was damaged in transit, please include photos of the packaging and item where possible — this helps us resolve the issue quickly.

Unwanted Items

Changed your mind? No problem — we accept returns for unwanted items within 30 days of receipt, provided they are returned in the same condition as sold.

If a new and sealed item has been opened, we will still accept the return, but the refund may be subject to a deduction based on the change in condition. Similarly, for pre-owned items, a deduction may apply if the item is returned in a condition different from how it was originally dispatched.

Additional Information

For full details, please refer to the eBay Money Back Guarantee and the Consumer Contract Regulations 2013, which protect your right to cancel within 14 days of receiving your order.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do you sell new or used games?

We sell both new and pre-owned games. Pre-owned games are graded from A to D based on their condition — you’ll find the Grade Marker and details in the product description.

Are the games complete?

Unless stated otherwise, all games are checked and complete. Where instructions are missing, we may include a reprint where possible. If an item is incomplete or has notable cosmetic damage, this will always be mentioned clearly by the Grade and Description. Typically we only sell items with notable damage or missing parts if it is vintage, collectible or rare.

Do you test electronic components?

Yes — any electronic parts are fully tested to ensure they’re working correctly. Please note that batteries are not included with pre-owned items to avoid issues with battery leakage.

What if my game is missing parts or damaged and it wasn’t mentioned?

This is very unlikely to happen, but if it does, please contact us right away. We take great care to check every game before listing to make sure all parts are present and the description is accurate. If we’ve missed something, we’ll make it right.

Do you sell spare parts for board games?

We keep a large stock of incomplete games to use for replacement parts, so if you’re missing something, we may be able to help! We don’t usually list spare parts individually due to postage costs, but if you need a specific piece, just get in touch — if we have it, we’ll gladly arrange it for you.

Do you offer trade-ins for board games?

Not yet — but we’re working on it! Stay tuned for updates as we develop our trade-in program.
