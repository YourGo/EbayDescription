Grading Guide

New
Brand new and unopened. The item and packaging are in perfect condition with no defects or signs of wear.

A Grade – Like New
Opened but unused. The item looks brand new and shows no signs of use. Packaging may have been unsealed but remains in excellent condition.

B Grade – Great
Lightly used and fully checked. The item is complete and in great condition, with only very minor signs of use or wear to the packaging.

C Grade – Good
Used but fully functional and complete. There may be visible wear or minor cosmetic damage to the box or components, which will be described in detail.

D Grade – Acceptable
Well-used or older items that still function as intended. There may be noticeable wear, replacement parts, or box damage. Typically applies to vintage or hard-to-find items.

For Parts / Incomplete
Items that are missing components or not fully functional. The description will clearly state what’s missing or affected.
